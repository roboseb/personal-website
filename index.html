About Me

Hey, I'm Seb. Most websites either suck or look like all the other sites. The tools we have access to are more powerful and performant than they've ever been, but we still seem to be stuck with an endless waste of boxes within boxes. I graduated high school dreading the idea of paying for university, so I didn't. I worked in warehouses most of the time between then and now, and even got my forklift ticket. As you know, forklift certification and web development go hand in hand, so my path was already laid out before me.

The connections became clearer and clearer as I progressed in my studies. The cold concrete floors became a clear parallel to the foundations of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Npm was the warehouse dock, with tech stacks being a complicated system of logistics. The precise forks of my lift were exchanged for the granular control that writing code grants. As the samurai of Japan had studied the blade, I had studied the forks, and all my skills transferred excellently to my new trade.

As a modern day ronin, I wander. In search of a path, a destination, and a sensei.
